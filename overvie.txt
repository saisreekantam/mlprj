Overview
Welcome to the SignalCluster Classification Challenge.

In this competition, your task is to develop a model that predicts the personality cluster of each observation based on two signal measurements: signal_strength and response_level.

Although the dataset appears simple at first glance, the patterns that separate the clusters may not be strictly linear. This makes the task a good opportunity to experiment with different classification techniques, visualizations, and decision boundary models.

Your goal is to correctly classify each sample into its corresponding Cluster category.

This is a multiclass classification problem, and submissions will be evaluated using Macro F1 Score, which ensures that all clusters are treated fairly regardless of their frequency.

Evaluation
Submissions are evaluated using Macro F1 Score:
    F1 marco = (1/K)(SUMMATION F1)

This metric gives equal importance to each cluster, regardless of how frequently it appears.
